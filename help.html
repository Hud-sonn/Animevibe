Help & Support

How do I add an anime to my watchlist?

Click the "+" or "Add to Watchlist" button on any anime card. It will be saved locally in your browser.

Why is my watchlist empty after refreshing?

Your watchlist is stored using localStorage. Make sure you’re using the same browser/device and haven’t cleared your site data.

How do I change the theme to light/dark?

Use the toggle button in the navigation to switch between light and dark themes. The preference is saved in your browser.

Can I upload a profile picture?

Yes! Go to the Profile page and select a photo under 1MB to update your profile picture (feature coming in v5).

How do I search for anime?

Use the search bar on the home page or genre pages. It filters cards in real-time based on title.

What if my watchlist disappears on another device?

Watchlist is local to the browser. For sync, use the same browser or export/import feature (coming soon).

How do I view trailers?

On genre pages, click "Toggle Details" on any card to expand and see the trailer iframe, description, and watchlist button.
