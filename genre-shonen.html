Attack on Titan

Humanity hides behind three massive walls to escape flesh-eating Titans. After a Titan breach destroys his hometown and kills his mother, Eren Yeager vows revenge and joins the elite Survey Corps with friends Mikasa and Armin. Battles reveal stunning truths: Eren can become a Titan, Titans are transformed humans, and their walled society—Paradis Island—is oppressed by the distant Marleyan Empire. Seeking freedom, Eren sparks a brutal world war and unleashes the apocalyptic Rumbling, marching countless Colossal Titans to trample the globe. Former comrades unite to stop him; Eren falls, ending the Rumbling and halting the cycle of hatred—though at immense human cost.
