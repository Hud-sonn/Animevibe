Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add anime to my watchlist?

Just click the “+ Watchlist” button on any anime card. It will be saved in your browser.

Will my data be saved if I close the site?

Yes, data is stored in your browser using localStorage and will remain unless you clear it manually.

Why isn't my anime list syncing across devices?

Currently, your data is only stored locally. It does not sync between browsers or devices.

Can I request new anime additions?

Yes! Use the contact form to suggest new anime, and we'll consider adding them in future updates.

Is there a mobile app?

The site is a PWA—add it to your home screen for app-like experience. Native app coming soon!

How do I toggle details on genre pages?

Click "Toggle Details" on any card to expand and see the description and trailer.

Why are trailers from YouTube?

We embed official trailers from YouTube for easy viewing. If a trailer is unavailable, let us know via contact.

Can I rate anime?

Rating system is planned for v5. For now, use the watchlist to save favorites.
